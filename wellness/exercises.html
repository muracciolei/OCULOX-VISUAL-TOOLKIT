About Focus Distance Exercises

Focus distance exercises help train the eye's accommodation system, which is responsible for adjusting focus between near and far objects. Regular practice can help reduce eye strain and maintain visual flexibility, especially beneficial for people who spend long periods focusing at close distances (computer work, reading).

Important: This is a simple exercise demonstration for UX exploration. For comprehensive eye care and vision therapy, always consult with qualified eye care professionals.
